Diploma in Horticulture and Agriculture Production

City College is delighted to offer a Diploma in Horticulture and Agriculture Production, a program designed to empower students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in modern agriculture and sustainable food production. This course integrates practical and theoretical training, preparing graduates to tackle the challenges of agricultural development and contribute to food security. The program spans three years, beginning at Level 4, advancing to Level 5, and culminating at Level 6, with each level comprising two semesters.

Program Structure

  • Academic Calendar: The academic year begins in October and concludes in August, with end-of-semester examinations to evaluate both academic and practical competencies.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Key areas of study include crop production, soil science, pest management, irrigation technology, agribusiness, and sustainable farming practices.

Why Choose Our Diploma in Horticulture and Agriculture Production?

1. Experienced Faculty

Our instructors are experts in agriculture and horticulture, combining extensive field experience with academic excellence to provide students with a well-rounded education.

2. Practical Field Training

Students gain hands-on experience through practical sessions, farm visits, and internships, learning to apply modern techniques to improve crop yields and farm efficiency.

3. Focus on Sustainability

The program emphasizes environmentally friendly practices and innovative technologies to ensure long-term agricultural success and sustainability.

4. Diverse Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as agricultural consultants, horticulturists, farm managers, extension officers, or entrepreneurs in agribusiness. The program also serves as a foundation for advanced studies in agricultural sciences.

Additional Benefits

  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: City College offers access to demonstration farms, greenhouses, and modern equipment to support hands-on learning.
  • Supportive Learning Environment: Students benefit from mentorship, interactive classes, and community engagement projects designed to enhance their skills and confidence.
  • Affordable Education: Our commitment to affordability ensures that aspiring agricultural professionals receive a quality education at a reasonable cost.

Enroll Today

By joining the Diploma in Horticulture and Agriculture Production at City College, students embark on a fulfilling journey toward a rewarding career in agriculture. Parents and guardians can trust that their children will receive comprehensive training in a supportive and professional environment.
